Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2025

Pic Reminder (“the App”) is developed and maintained by Davecy LLC.

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

a. Photos

  • The App allows you to capture photos using your device camera or select photos from your photo library.
  • Photos are stored locally on your device or in your device’s photo library.
  • We do not upload, transmit, or store photos on external servers.

b. Reminders & Notes

  • Reminder dates, times, and optional text notes are stored locally on your device.
  • This data is used only to provide reminder functionality.

c. Notifications

  • The App uses local notifications to remind you at scheduled times.
  • Notification scheduling data never leaves your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data linked to your identity

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use system-level services provided by the operating system (Camera, Photo Library, Local Notifications). These services are used only to provide core app functionality and are governed by Apple’s privacy policies.

The App does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

4. Data Sharing

  • Your data is never shared, sold, rented, or transferred to third parties.
  • All content remains under your control on your device.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • All reminders and photos remain on your device until you delete them.
  • Deleting a reminder in the App removes its associated data and cancels its notification.

6. Security

We rely on the security features provided by your device and operating system. Since no data is transmitted externally, risk exposure is minimized.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, compliance risk is minimal.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect app changes or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
